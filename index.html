Weightlist

Weightlist helps you lose weight and develop lifelong healthy habits by completing challenges in 4 different categories.

Log items to lists such as foods, workouts, recipes, and weights as you complete 40 different challenges.

Track your progress and view stats along the way.

When the app becomes available, talk to your doctor and make sure they think you are a good candidate to use the Weightlist app!
